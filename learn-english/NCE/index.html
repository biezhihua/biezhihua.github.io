    表达形式

    我会逐句分析每篇课文，为了一致性，下面约定一些表达形式。

    句子成分（使用[target]的形式作为单词的后缀来表示句子成分类型）:

    • the different parts of speech in English:
      • [conj] 连词
      • [noun] 名词
      • [adjective] 形容词
      • [adverb] 副词
      • [frequency adverb] 频度副词
      • [time adverb] 时间副词
      • [be verb] be动词
      • [link verb] 系动词
      • [auxiliary verb] 助动词
      • [prepositional object phrase] 介宾短语
    • the parts of a sentence in English:
      • [there be] there be
      • [subject] 主语
      • [predicate verb phrase] 谓语动词短语
      • [verb phrase] 动词短语
      • [not] 否定
      • [auxiliary verb] 助动词
      • [object] 宾语
      • [direct object] 直接宾语
      • [indirect object] 间接宾语
      • [object complement] 宾语补语
      • [noun complement] 名词补语
      • [noun phrase] 名词短语
      • [subject complement] 主语补语
      • [predicative] 表语
      • [attributive] 定语
      • [appositive] 同位语
      • [non-predicate as object] 非谓语做宾语
      • [non-predicate] 非谓语
      • [to-do as object] 不定式作宾语
      • [to-do as verb complement] 不定式作动词补语

      • [to-do as predicative complement] 不定式作表语补语
      • [to-do as attributive] 不定式做定语
      • [to-do as predicative] 不定式做表语
      • [to-do as adverbial of purpose] 不定式做目的状语
      • [doing as attributive] 动名词做定语
      • [doing as subject] 动名词做主语
      • [doing as object] 动名词做宾语
      • [doing as adverbial of time] 动名词做时间状语
      • [doing as adverbial of cause] 动名词做原因状语
      • [doing as adverbial of manner] 动名词做方式状语

      • [doing as adverbial] 动名词做状语
      • [doing] 动名词
      • [doing as subject complement] 动名词做主语补语
      • [done as adverbial of manner] 过去分词做方式状语
      • [done as adverbial of accompany] 过去分词做伴随状语
      • [done as attributive] 过去分词做定语
      • [adverbial] 谓语动词短语的状语
        • [adverbial of predicate verb phrase] = [adverbial of verb phrase] = [adverbial]
      • [adverbial of concession] 让步状语
      • [adverbial of place] 地点状语
      • [adverbial of time] 时间状语
      • [adverbial of manner] 方式状语
      • [adverbial of purpose] 目的状语
      • [adverbial of accompany] 伴随状语
      • [passive form of verb] 被动语态
      • [parenthesis] 插入语
    • tense:
      • [present simple tense] 现在一般时
      • [present continuous tense] 现在进行时
      • [present perfect tense] 现在完成时
      • [present perfect continuous tense] 现在完成进行时
      • [past simple tense] 过去一般时
      • [past continuous tense] 过去进行时
      • [past perfect tense] 过去完成时
      • [past future tense] 过去将来时
      • [future simple tense] 将来一般时
      • [future continuous tense] 将来进行时
      • [future perfect tense] 将来完成时
      • [future simple continuous tense] 将来一般进行时
    • clause:
      • [attributive clause] 定语从句
      • [object clause] 宾语从句
      • [predicative clause] 表语从句
      • [adverbial clause of time] 时间状语从句
      • [adverbial clause of cause] 原因状语从句
      • [adverbial clause of condition] 条件状语从句
      • [adverbial clause of concession] 让步状语从句
      • [adverbial clause of comparison] 比较状语从句
      • [adverbial clause of result] 结果状语从句
      • [noun clause] 名词性从句
      • [appositive clause] 同位语从句
    • sentence type:
      • simple sentence 简单句
      • simple question sentence 一般疑问句
      • compound sentence 并列句
      • complex sentence 复杂句

    例如：

    Last week I went to the theatre.
- simple sentence:
    - Last week [adverbial of the predicate verb]
    - I [subject]
    - went [predicate verb phrase]
    - to the theatre [prepositional objective phrase]
